ec45.klymot.com

Daily archive of ECMWF open-data IFS ensemble 2 m temperature forecasts (00z run, 15-day horizon), sampled at the 27,961 GHCN station locations shown on www.klymot.com.

Start at data/manifest.json. Layout, formats, and capture code: the repository.
